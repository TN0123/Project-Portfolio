What sections should the site have?

Select all that you want to keep. Think about what actually matters to someone landing on your site.

1

Hero / Intro

Name, one-liner about who you are, maybe links to GitHub/LinkedIn/email. First impression.

2

About

Short bio — background, what you're into, what you're working on. Not a resume dump.

3

Experience

Work experience — Google (incoming), Capital One, SociableAI, IBM, UMD TA. Clean timeline or cards.

4

Projects

Featured projects with descriptions and links. Maybe curate down from the current 10 to your best 4-6.

5

Skills / Tech

Technologies you work with. But way cleaner than the current massive tag dump — grouped and minimal.

6

Contact

Email + social links. Simple footer-style or its own small section.
